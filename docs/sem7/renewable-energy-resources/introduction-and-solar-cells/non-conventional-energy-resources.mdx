# Non Conventional Energy Resources

## Conventional Energy Resources

These are the sources of energy which are exhaustible i.e., cannot be replaced if once they are used<br/>
**Example:** Coal, Petroleum products, Natural gas, etc.

## Non Conventional (Renewable) Energy Sources

These are the sources of energy which are inexhaustible i.e., can be used to produce energy again and again<br/>
**Example:** Sub, Water, Animal dung, Agro-waste etc.
